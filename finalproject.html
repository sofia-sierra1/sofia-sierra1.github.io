Sofia Sierra and Ava Stanley Final Project

I am Sofia Sierra and my partner for this project is Ava Stanley. Together, we created a coloring book program. To begin coloring,
all you have to do is hit the start button. This will bring you to your first coloring page. There are colors displayed at the botton of
the screen and all you have to do to set it as the color you would like to use is click it. Once you have completed coloring the page,
you may hit the next page button.
